About

I am receiving my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics & Computer Science from √âcole Polytechnique this June 2020.

For as long as I can remember, I've had a passion for mathematics. I actually learned to do multiplications and divisions before I learned to read! It was not until my undergraduate studies that I discovered and quickly became obsessed with programming. Music is also a life-long passion of mine, and also what introduced me to AI. My worlds collided when I used LSTMs to generate music for my second-year programming project .

I fell in love with NLP during my summer internship at Illuin Technology. Unwilling to let go of the project I started after the end of my internship, I am currently working as a freelance researcher for Illuin and researching unsupervised question answering.

Research

My goal is to create NLP solutions that are generalizable, usable and beneficial.

I aim to employ multilingual models and unsupervised learning to develop approaches generalizable to low-resource languages and specific domains. I want to improve how machines understand our language, so that using modern technology is as easy as maintaing a conversation for anyone. I am determined to leverage NLP to identify injustice in social contexts like law enforcement or employment, and address bias towards marginalized populations.

With this goal in mind, my next large-scale project is my Bachelor‚Äôs thesis on neural machine translation of sign language glosses to natural language.
